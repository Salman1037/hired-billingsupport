Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What services do you offer? +

We specialize in UI/UX design, frontend development, motion design, and brand strategy. Our goal is to create digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional.

How long does a project take? +

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A typical website project takes 4-8 weeks from start to finish.

Do you offer ongoing support? +

Yes! We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website stays up-to-date and performs at its best.

What technologies do you use? +

We use modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), GSAP for animations, and Lenis for smooth scrolling. We avoid frameworks like React or Bootstrap unless explicitly requested.
